1 JOHN IN ESL FORMAT

The Book of 1 John has been adapted for an most interesting ESL course. It is given in its entirety (over 40 pages as a PDF document) following the list of other ESL material.

Contact Viola Pahl via E-mail for more information on the following exciting topics. All lesson material has graphics included to enhance the learning process for the students. (viola@pahl.ca)
The Abominable English Language
Klondike Gold
Last Rights, Last Rites, Last Writes
The Five Senses: Touch, Taste, Hearing, Seeing, Touching
each topic has its own set of lesson material
Granny's Love/Hate Affair with a Computer
Jewels - many interesting stories about jewels
Colours of the World - about various ethnic backgrounds

There are also 6 mystery stories. A mystery story is divided into sections, with the students getting just one section at a time so they cannot read the ending until the last handout. In each section there is planted at least three grammatical/spelling mistakes, and students are the "Grammar Detectives" to see how many they can spot.
